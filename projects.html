Projects

Semi-professional development

These projects are going to be either development I did for school/work, in competitive teams, or simply for personal development/progression, rather than strictly creative or personal need projects.

Fly Aware

Fly Aware was a chrome extension built for the Earth and Atmospheric Sciences college at Georgia Tech. It helps users track the carbon footprint of flights that they are planning to take, gives them contextual understandings of their emissions, then allows them the option to offset them.

Learn more

Union Pacific Alexa Bot

For a hackathon while working at Union Pacific, a team and I wrote an Alexa/text bot that would take in information requests from UP customers, check a live updating database, and relay the appropriate information back. Unfortunately, I don't have a way to distribute the project, as it existed in several different servers and development tools simultaneously.

Homeless Shelter Booking App

As part of a group, I developed over the course of a software engineering team class an Android application. The simple idea is that homeless individuals would be able to check their phones, see how many beds were available at local shelters, and reserve one if necessary, distributing the supply more evenly.

Dr Homeobox

Dr Homeobox is the first game I made that I'm okay calling a game. Completed as part of a game jam, done in 48 hours, and woefully buggy and short. Still think the concept is really neat, and can definitely serve as a prototype, and encouragement to do this more. Art/concept done by my sister.

Arduino Controller for LED strip

Something that I've always wanted is mood lighting that I can control from anywhere in the house. Best solution I came up with is buying a WS2812B strip, an esp8266, and wiring them together. Simply use this code, and control them through your network! Use the Python server for additional convenience.
