Project Euler Solutions

Simple, commented, effective solutions to Project Euler problems as presented by Hackerrank.

Why do this?

I found Project Euler through a friend, and it was initially a competition to solve as many as possible in a short period of time. It was fun, but also really got me into the problems. They keep my brain engaged, challenge me in directions I wouldn't necessarily have to go otherwise, and help keep me up to speed in computer science and mathy sort of issues. They're also fun to complete when I'm bored and don't want to focus on homework, it's like solving puzzles, and getting all the green checkmarks on Hackerrank keeps me happy. I've also found it's an effective way to learn Python, as I have to learn various Python abilities, actually code in it, and get a better handling on a more scripting focused approach to programming.
